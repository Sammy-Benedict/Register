Support & Help

Get answers to common questions and contact support

📝 How do I complete my registration?

Go to the Registration tab, fill in your personal and academic information, upload required documents, and proceed to payment. Your registration will be confirmed once payment is verified.

💰 What payment methods are accepted?

We accept mobile money payments via MTN Mobile Money, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo Money, and Telecel Cash. Payment is processed securely through Paystack.

📄 What documents are required?

You will need to upload: National ID/Student ID, proof of admission, and a recent passport photograph. Acceptable formats are PDF, JPG, and PNG (max 5MB each).

💳 How much is the registration fee?

The registration fee depends on your student level: Freshers: GHS 350 | Continuing Students (200L+): GHS 400

✅ When will my registration be confirmed?

Your registration is typically confirmed within 24 hours of successful payment and document verification. You'll receive an email confirmation.

🔐 Is my information secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and secure payment gateways. Your personal information is never shared with third parties.
